About Us

What is Plate2Purpose

A smart web platform that helps reduce food wastage in schools by tracking attendance, planning meals, collecting feedback, and aligning kitchen preparation with real demand.

Why We Exist

Plate2Purpose was created to support Sustainable Development Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production. Our mission is to turn every meal into a meaningful action by minimizing waste and maximizing awareness.

The Problem We’re Solving

Every day, schools waste food due to over-preparation, poor planning, and lack of communication. This wastes not just food, but time, money, and effort — all of which could be saved with smart technology.

How Plate2Purpose Helps

By combining real-time attendance tracking, dynamic meal calculation, UPI-based payment logging, and a daily menu system, we help school staff prepare exactly what’s needed — no more, no less. Students get better meals, kitchens waste less, and the planet wins.
